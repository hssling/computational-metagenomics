Dear Editor,

Please consider the enclosed manuscript, **"Computational Metagenomics in Practice: A Tutorial Roadmap from 16S rRNA Amplicon Profiling to Shotgun Sequencing and Compositional Data Analysis,"** for publication as a tutorial review in *Briefings in Bioinformatics*.

The manuscript was prepared to address a persistent gap in microbiome research training: many investigators now have access to sequencing but not to a coherent analytical framework that links study design, read processing, taxonomic and functional interpretation, and statistically appropriate inference. The article provides a structured and publication-oriented roadmap spanning 16S rRNA amplicon analysis, shotgun metagenomics, genome-resolved workflows, and compositional data analysis. It also emphasizes common analytical errors, minimum reporting expectations, and reproducibility safeguards.

This submission is intended as an educational and methodological synthesis rather than a report of a new experimental dataset. The figures are clearly labeled as tutorial schematics or formatted illustrative examples created for explanatory purposes. No part of the manuscript has been published previously, and the manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere.
